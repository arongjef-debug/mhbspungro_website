About School

Mount Horeb Baptist School is a renowned educational institution for center of learning located at the heart of pungro town.

Laid a Foundation and dedicated on 16th of feb 2009.

It is also the youngest established school under pungro sub-division.

Significant of the school name:

The founders of our school believed in education for all. Their dream was to build a community that values integrity, compassion, and excellence in learning.The name of the school was taken from the Bible reference, As according to the Bible mention, Mount Horeb is called 'the Mount of God', this mountain is an institution of God on which the Lord appeared to Moses in the form of flame and instructed him to lead the people of God and gave him the power to lead the people of Israel from the bondage to the freedom towards the promised land. In another reference, The Lord also appeared to Prophet. Elijah on the same mountain and gave him a clear direction to select and appoint the leaders both in religion and political kingdom.

     Thus, with a hopeful expectation that this institution will produce many politicians, bureaucrats, technocrats, missionaries and other professionals in the long run.

Logo:

   1. Mountain-symbolizes the mountainous region of pungro sub-division specifically refers to the Mt. Saramati regions

   2. An open book-symbolizes the academic excellence in the future

   3. Sunlight-represents the bright future ahead.

   4. Mountain covered in green color-symbolizes the prosperity of our future through education.

   5. Motto-''LEARN TO LEAD AND SHINE''

   6. Vission-To foster the best in every student by providing a holistic education and inspiring environment

   7. Mission:-

          i. Creating environment for diverse learning strategies, motivation and self discipline.

          ii. Develop life skills, leadership qualities, perseverance and a sense of integrity in all students by creating opportunities.
